The drive link contains the jupyter notebook of the classifier and the dataset.

1. Upload the jupyter notebook(file with .ipynb extension) in google colab and the dataset(file with .csv extension) on google drive.
2. After running the second cell, your google drive should have been mounted and now visible on the left hand sidebar.
3. Locate the uploaded dataset in the directory of your drive and right click on the file, copy the path and paste it in <dataset_link> in pd.read_csv("<dataset_link>") in the third cell.
4. Now we are ready to run all our remaining cells.
5. In our cell "PREPROCESSING THE QUERY", we can set our required query.
6. Our final cell gives the most similar documents.
